RISING MOMS

Helping young teen mothers who have dropped out of school because of early teenage pregnancy

About Us

RISING MOMS is an NGO that helps young teen mothers who have dropped out of school because of early teenage pregnancy. We provide them with the support they need to get back on track, including:

  • Education and job training
  • Childcare
  • Mental health counseling
  • Financial assistance

Our Services

We offer a variety of services to help young teen mothers get back on track, including:

  • Education and job training
  • Childcare
  • Mental health counseling
  • Financial assistance

We also offer a variety of support groups and activities, such as:

  • Mommy and me groups
  • Parenting classes
  • Social events

How to Get Help

If you are a young teen mother who has dropped out of school, we can help. Please contact us today to learn more about our services.

You can contact us by phone at 1-800-555-1212 or by email at info@risingmoms.org.
